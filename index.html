Ivan Konstantinovich Aivazovsky

Russian marine painter, batalist, collector, philanthropist. The painter of the Main Naval Staff, academician and honorary member of the Imperial Academy of Arts, honorary member of the Academy of Arts in Amsterdam, Rome, Paris, Florence and Stuttgart.

1817 - 1900
The great marine painter Ivan Konstantinovich Aivazovsky was born on July 17, 1817 into a family of market elders and craftswomen, embroiderers. Before the youngest son appeared in the family of Konstantin and Hripsime Aivazovsky, four offspring had already been brought up: three girls and a boy named Sargis. At the baptism of the future celebrity was given the name of Hovhannes, who later acquired the Slavic version of Ivan.
